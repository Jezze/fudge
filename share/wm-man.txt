MANUAL: WINDOW MANAGER

Keyboard bindings:

Alt+[1-8]:              Change workspace
Alt+Shift+[1-8]:        Change workspace and bring focused window
Alt+h:                  Decrease main area
Alt+j:                  Move focus to previous window
Alt+k:                  Move focus to next window
Alt+l:                  Increase main area
Alt+Enter:              Move focused window to main area
Alt+Shift+p:            Open a new shell
Alt+Shift+q:            Close active window
Alt+Shift+z:            Exit window manager
